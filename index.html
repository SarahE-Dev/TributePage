Timeline of John's Musical Career:

2006 Officially joins the band Be Your Own Pet, replacing their drummer and going on tour with them.

2007 Producer, Drummer and Composer for Turbo Fruits

2009 Plays all instruments and writes music for Jemina Pearl's solo album and tour.

2009 Plays the drummer of the band in the movie Whip It, directed by Drew Barrymore.

2010 Officially joins The Virgins as their drummer and goes on tour with them.

2011 Vocalist and Drummer for Smith Westerns

2013 Plays guitar for Eleanor Friedberger

2014 Starts the band Public Access TV

2019 Starts Solo Career as John Eatherly / Club Intl.

2020 Plays drums for the Cults

2022 Starts work on New Album and Tour for Be Your Own Pet Reunion
